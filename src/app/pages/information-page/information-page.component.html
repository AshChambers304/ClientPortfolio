Michelle Gomes Interiors is a creative Interior Design studio in Stafford, Midlands, United Kingdom. We collaborate with clients to create their dream residential and commercial spaces fulfilling all their requirements and aspirations. The results are beautiful functional spaces that improve the quality of lives of the people that enjoy the spaces and reflect their personalities.

Michelle is an experienced interior designer who graduated with distinction from the British Academy of Interior Design and is an Associate of the British Institute of Interior Design. She is passionate about Interiors, Interior Architecture, Furniture and Design.

Her expertise has been gained by delivering residential and commercial projects utilising her network of skilled suppliers in the industry; and previously working for an award-winning Interior Designer in London.
